# New Zealand Industry Revenue Dashboard (2016–2025)

Interactive Power BI dashboard analysing New Zealand industry revenue trends and distribution using Stats NZ business financial data.

## Dashboard Preview
<img width="1305" height="735" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3217bbab-b382-475a-89c3-59a74da8be89" />

## Key Insights
- Revenue trend by year (2016–2025)
- Top 10 industries by revenue
- Revenue share (Top 10 industries)
- Filters: Year range and Industry

## Tools & Skills
- Power BI (data modelling, visualisations)
- Power Query (data cleaning & date transformation)
- DAX measures (Total Revenue, Latest Year Revenue)

## Data Source
Stats NZ – Business financial data (CSV download).
